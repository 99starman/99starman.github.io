I am a MSCS student at Georgia Tech. I received my BSc degree in Computer Science and Statistics from University of British Columbia in the beautiful Vancouver, Canada.

My research interests are Trustworthy and Data-Centric ML, focusing on building safe, socially-aware, and data-efficient Foundation Models and their applications. Specifically, I am interested in:

(1) Safety and Value Alignment for Foundation Models.

(2) Benchmarking and mitigating model reliability issues, including faithfulness/fidelity, privacy/memorization and truthfulness/calibration.

(3) Data synthesis for model post-training.

As of Summer 2025, I am a Research Engineer Intern at the Center for AI Safety. Previously, I was a research assistant at Dartmouth College where I had the chance to work with Dr. Ruibo Liu and Prof. Soroush Vosoughi on Tool-augmented Language Models and Value Alignment for LLMs.
