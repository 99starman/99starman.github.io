I am an incoming MSCS student at Georgia Tech. I received my BSc degree in Computer Science and Statistics from University of British Columbia in the beautiful Vancouver, Canada.

My research interests lie in the field of Trustworthy and Social NLP, with the goal of building safe, reliable and socially-aware language models and applications. Specifically, I am interested in (1) Aligning large language models with diverse human preferences and social values; (2) Addressing the limitations of current LLMs that undermine their reliability, such as privacy leakage and uncertainty miscalibration; (3) Human-centered LLM evaluation.

During my undergrad years, I was a research assistant at Dartmouth College where I had the chance to work with Dr. Ruibo Liu and Prof. Soroush Vosoughi on Tool-augmented Language Models and LLM Alignment.
